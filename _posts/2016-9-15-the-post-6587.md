# Concepts of Tensor

## Definition
Tensor can be viewed as the high order generalization of matrix.

## Tensor Rank
If tensor can be decomposed into a linear combination of **decomposable tensors D(r)**:
$$ T = \sum_{r=1}^{R} {\lambda_r D(r)}$$
